
## How it works

This is a VGA Demo that draws a Galton peg board and "drops" soft paint balls (they "squish" at the bottom) onto the pegboard.  
It also has audio output when the balls hit the pegs if the Audio PMOD is connected (output on uio_out[7]).

![](galton_frame.png)

## How to test

Connect the VGA Pmod and enjoy.  Optionally connect audio PMOD.

## External hardware

VGA Pmod.
Optional Audio PMOD (output is on uio_out[7]
