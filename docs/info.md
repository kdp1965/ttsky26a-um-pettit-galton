## How it works

This is a VGA Demo that draws a Galton peg board and "drops" soft metal steel balls (they "squish" at the bottom) onto the pegboard.

## How to test

Connect the VGA Pmod and enjoy

## External hardware

VGA Pmod. Optional Gamepad PMOD
